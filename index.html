The Foundation

The Exposure
Triangle

Every photo is controlled by three settings working together. Change one and you'll need to adjust the others to keep the image looking right.

Setting 01

Aperture

The size of the lens opening — controls light & background blur
f / 1.8Blurry background · More light
f / 5.6Some blur · Balanced
f / 16Sharp everywhere · Less light
Small f = Wide open
Large f = Narrow
Setting 02

Shutter Speed

How long the sensor is exposed to light
Fast
1/2000sFreezes all motion
Medium
1/250sGood for everyday shots
Slow
1/4sSilky water effect
Very Slow
10–30sLight trails · Use tripod
Setting 03

ISO

Sensor sensitivity to light — trade-off is grain
ISO 100Clean · Outdoors
ISO 1600Some grain · Indoors
ISO 6400Grainy · Night
Low ISO = Clean
High ISO = Grain
